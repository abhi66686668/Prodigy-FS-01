Our Story

Founded in 2010, Skanda Grocery began as a small neighborhood store with a simple mission: to provide fresh, high-quality groceries at fair prices. What started as a single storefront has grown into a beloved local chain serving thousands of happy customers across the region.

Our commitment to quality, customer service, and community values has remained unchanged. We work directly with local farmers and trusted suppliers to bring you the best products while supporting our local economy.

Today, we're proud to offer both in-store shopping and convenient online delivery, making it easier than ever for our customers to access fresh, wholesome groceries.
